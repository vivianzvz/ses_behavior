Welcome

Thank you for joining this study. First, you will complete a short 3-paged background questionnaire about yourself. After that, you will take part in a series of six interactive decision tasks alongside another anonymous participant.

In these tasks, you will sometimes see a brief summary of the other participant's questionnaire responses. All survey answers remain fully anonymous and cannot be traced back to you personally.

Please answer each question as honestly and accurately as you can. There is a 90 second time limit on each page. Click “Next” when you’re ready to begin and move onto the next page.
